About Us

We are a small local business founded in 1976. We opperate on a small scale and only have a 6 person staff. We pride ourselves on creating delicious sushi with yummy combinations of flavors, textures, and colors. Each ingredient is always fresh and never frozen. Living in Kona, on the Big Island makes fresh seafood easily accessible.

We hope to see you stop by for some reasonably priced, delicous sushi near the waters edge.
